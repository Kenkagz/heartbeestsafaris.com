Special Services For Heartbeest Safaris Visitors

Car Parking
At Heartbeest Safaris, we understand the importance of convenient and accessible parking for our guests. Whether you're stopping by our offices to plan your safari adventure or attending one of our exclusive events, we’ve got you covered. On-Site Parking: We provide ample parking space at our Hurlingham office location, ensuring your vehicle is secure while you focus on planning your dream safari. Accessible Parking: Designated accessible parking spaces are available for guests with specific mobility needs. Nearby Parking Options: In the event our on-site parking is full, there are nearby parking facilities within walking distance for your convenience. Safe and Secure: All parking areas are monitored to ensure the safety of your vehicle. Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions about parking or need assistance upon arrival. We’re here to make your visit as smooth as possible!
Animal Photos
At Heartbeest Safaris, we believe every moment spent with nature is a moment to cherish. Our animal photos capture the essence of Kenya’s breathtaking wildlife, showcasing creatures in their natural habitats. Majestic Creatures: From the mighty elephants to the graceful cheetahs, our photos highlight the strength, beauty, and elegance of Kenya’s wildlife. Incredible Moments: Every image tells a unique story — a lion’s fierce gaze, a giraffe's serene elegance, or a zebra’s playful stride. Natural Beauty: Our photos remind you of the unspoiled landscapes and the rich biodiversity that make Kenya one of the most captivating destinations on Earth. Conservation Focus: We are dedicated to preserving these majestic creatures, ensuring that future generations can experience the same awe-inspiring wildlife we encounter today. Let our photos transport you into the heart of Kenya, where wildlife and wilderness come alive in their most authentic form.
Guide Services
At Heartbeest Safaris, we provide expert guide services that ensure you experience the best of Kenya's wildlife, landscapes, and cultural heritage. Whether you're on a safari adventure, exploring hidden gems, or discovering local traditions, our knowledgeable guides are here to enhance your journey. Expert Guides: Our team of highly trained and experienced guides are passionate about Kenya's wildlife and ecosystems. They provide in-depth knowledge, ensuring you gain a deeper understanding of the animals, plants, and natural wonders around you. Personalized Safari Experience: Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, our guides tailor each safari experience to suit your interests and preferences, making your adventure truly unique. Cultural Insights: Our guides not only show you the wonders of the wildlife but also share fascinating stories about the local cultures, traditions, and history, enriching your travel experience. Safety and Comfort: Your safety and comfort are our top priority. Our guides are well-versed in the safe interaction with wildlife and the terrain, ensuring a smooth and secure journey. Local Expertise: As locals, our guides offer insider knowledge of the best spots for animal sightings, scenic views, and hidden treasures, helping you explore Kenya like never before.
Food & Beverages
At Heartbeest Safaris, we understand that a great adventure is paired with refreshing beverages to keep you energized and hydrated throughout your journey. Whether you're enjoying a sunrise safari or relaxing at our office after a day of planning, we offer a variety of beverages to suit your tastes. Refreshing Beverages: From chilled soft drinks to fresh juices, we ensure you stay refreshed as you explore the wonders of the wild. Hot Drinks: Enjoy a warm cup of coffee or tea while taking in the morning sunrise or during a relaxing break after your safari adventure. Local Specialties: Savor a taste of local flavors with our selection of traditional Kenyan beverages, including fresh coconut water, sugarcane juice, or homemade herbal teas. Signature Safari Drinks: For those special moments, we offer signature drinks designed to complement the safari experience, whether you're toasting a successful day of exploration or simply unwinding. Hydration on the Go: Our team makes sure you have access to clean water during your safari to keep you hydrated and feeling your best during your adventure. Whether you're in need of a refreshing sip or a warm drink to unwind, we've got you covered every step of the way. Cheers to an unforgettable experience!
Souvenir Shopping
At Heartbeest Safaris, we believe that the memories of your journey should be celebrated and cherished long after your safari ends. Our souvenir shopping experience offers a wide range of unique, high-quality items that reflect the beauty and culture of Kenya. Authentic Kenyan Craftsmanship: Browse a curated selection of handcrafted items made by local artisans, from intricate jewelry and beadwork to vibrant textiles and traditional art. Each piece tells a story of Kenya's rich cultural heritage. Safari Mementos: Bring home a piece of the wild with safari-themed souvenirs like animal figurines, photographs, and apparel that capture the spirit of your adventure. Eco-Sunendly Products: We are committed to sustainability, offering eco-conscious souvenirs made from recycled materials or sustainably sourced resources, allowing you to support both local craftsmanship and conservation efforts. Personalized Gifts: Find the perfect gift to remember your journey, whether it's a custom item engraved with your safari date or a one-of-a-kind creation to commemorate your experience. Unique Local Finds: Explore a variety of locally made products, from handwoven baskets to carved wooden statues, each representing the diverse cultures of the regions you visit. Whether you're looking for a meaningful keepsake or a special gift for a loved one, our souvenir shopping experience allows you to take a piece of Kenya home with you.

Camping Ground
At Heartbeest Safaris, we offer an unforgettable camping experience that brings you closer to the wild, all while ensuring comfort and safety. Whether you're a seasoned camper or new to the experience, our camping grounds provide the perfect setting for an immersive adventure. Scenic Camping Locations: Our carefully selected camping sites are set in breathtaking locations, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscapes and wildlife, so you can wake up to the sounds of nature every day. Comfortable Setup: Enjoy the convenience of well-equipped campsites with tents, comfortable bedding, and essentials provided, so you can focus on enjoying your adventure without worrying about setup. Authentic Safari Experience: Sleep under the stars, surrounded by nature, and experience the magic of the Kenyan wilderness firsthand, all while maintaining a safe distance from the wild. Amenities & Facilities: Our camping grounds offer necessary amenities such as clean restrooms, showers, and communal areas to ensure a comfortable stay. Guided Campfire Nights: End your day with a guided campfire session where you can share stories, enjoy a warm meal, and stargaze in the clear Kenyan sky, creating lasting memories with fellow adventurers. Safety & Security: We prioritize your safety, with trained staff on-site, secure camping areas, and proper precautions to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience throughout your stay. Experience the magic of camping in Kenya with Heartbeest Safaris — where comfort meets adventure in the heart of the wilderness.
Rest House
At Heartbeest Safaris, we offer cozy and welcoming rest houses that provide a perfect retreat after a day of exploring the Kenyan wilderness. Whether you're looking for a peaceful getaway or a comfortable base to recharge during your safari, our rest houses offer a blend of relaxation and convenience. Comfortable Accommodations: Our rest houses are thoughtfully designed to offer a relaxing environment with comfortable beds, modern amenities, and scenic views, ensuring you have a restful stay. Tranquil Setting: Nestled in serene locations, our rest houses provide the ideal escape from the hustle and bustle, allowing you to unwind in peace while being close to nature’s wonders. Home Away From Home: Enjoy the warmth and hospitality of our well-maintained rest houses, featuring all the comforts you need for a peaceful stay, including hot showers, Wi-Fi, and cozy lounges for relaxing after a day of adventure. Scenic Views: Each rest house offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes, allowing you to enjoy the beauty of Kenya from the comfort of your home base. Fresh Meals & Refreshments: Indulge in freshly prepared meals that capture the local flavors of the region, served with a selection of drinks to enhance your stay. Easy Access to Safari Activities: Our rest houses are conveniently located, allowing for easy access to safari activities, guided tours, and local attractions, making it the perfect place to rest between your adventures. Whether you're seeking solitude, adventure, or both, our rest houses offer a peaceful haven to relax, recharge, and enjoy your safari experience to the fullest.
